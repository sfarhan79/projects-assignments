Business Objective:

Predict the Stock Price for the next 30 days. There are Open, High, Low and Close prices for day starting from 2000 until 2022 for Reliance Industries.

● Split the last year into a test set- to build a model to predict stock price.
● Find short term, & long term trends.
● Understand how it is impacted from external factors or any big external events.
● Forecast for next 1 year.

Perform:
1. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
a. Describing the dataset
b. Cleaning the data
c. Checking invalid records
d. Missing value detection and imputation
e. Duplicated records
f. Outliers
g. Feature Engineering
h. Data Visualization

2. Model building
a. Identifying algorithms, building model/s, testing and evaluation.

3. Model Deployment
a. Utilization of pickle 
b. Leveraging Flask framework
c. Deploying via Stream-lit
